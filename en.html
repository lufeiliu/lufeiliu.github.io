About me

As an NLP engineer with a master's degree in NLP, I bring a wealth of experience in text structure analysis and text generation within the patent domain, actively contributing to the entire lifecycle of NLP projects. My dual role as a freelance translator and interpreter underscores the significance of swiftly gathering crucial information for mission preparation, fostering a deep interest in information retrieval and text summarization. I take pride in the seamless synergy between my roles, finding accomplishment in the complementary nature of NLP engineering and language translation. My inherent sensitivity to language, analytical prowess, and unwavering passion for emerging technologies continually drive my pursuit of excellence in the NLP domain, motivating me to evolve and contribute meaningfully to both fields.
