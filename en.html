About me

I am a Natural Language Processing (NLP) engineer with experience in text structure analysis and text generation in the field of patents. My professional journey has allowed me to work on the entire life cycle of NLP projects, from defining use cases and selecting data and models to deployment. Additionally, as a freelance translator and interpreter, I have developed an ability to quickly gather relevant information for the preparation of my assignments. These experiences have strengthened my interest in information retrieval and automatic summarization in NLP. My passion for languages, analytical skills, and enthusiasm for new technologies continue to drive me to expand my expertise in the field of NLP.
